Know Your Way Around ULK

We are in the process of developing a mapping system that is all-inclusive
and serves to improve the environment and the campus at Kigali Independent
University. Our purpose is to make the students, teachers, and the visitors have
an easy time navigating the campus. To this end, what we are focused on
accomplishing is an intelligent software-based map that will be the students,
faculty members, and guests' sides in their navigation through the campus.
Our green purpose is to not only offer better solutions but also promote
sustainable innovation by integrating digital technologies to provide
reliable and user-friendly maps, interactive tours, and location information
via digital signages among other services.
